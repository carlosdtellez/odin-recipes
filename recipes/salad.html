Italian-Style Tuna Salad

Description

Italian Tuna Salad loaded with fresh vegetables, garlic, parsley and olives. The no mayo tuna salad is perfect for those who are not a fan of mayonnaise in their tuna salad. This low carb tuna recipe is not only delicious and nutritious, the delicious Mediterranean tuna is also Keto friendly

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 slices Italian bread, cubed
  • 3 tomatoes, cut into small pieces
  • 1 medium cucumber, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 1 small onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 (5 ounce) can tuna, drained
  • 20 black olives
  • 1 tablespoon capers

Steps

  1. Mix olive oil, red wine vinegar, salt, and pepper together for the dressing.

  2. Mix bread, tomatoes, cucumber, onion, tuna, olives, and capers together gently. Pour dressing on top and serve.
